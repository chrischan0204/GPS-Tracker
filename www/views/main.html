To Use
  1. Register here
    Don't forget your password as there's no reset yet
  2. Once logged in, add your device's IMEI
    Get this by SMSing the device, instructions in manual
  3. Tell your device to start using gateway 59.167.194.239:9000

  • Use of the system is entirely at your own risk, I take no responsibility for ... anything
